Inside the book

10X YOUR LIFE: ULTIMATE MASTERCLASS
From Alarm Clock to Alpha — A Self-Help Guide for the Unhinged

Description:

What if the secret to success isn’t balance, but chaos? What if you’ve been lied to about discipline, comfort, and “self-care”? This is not your average self-help book. This is a full-throttle, no-snooze-button manifesto for anyone bold (or delusional) enough to chase greatness like it owes them money.

Inside, you’ll learn:

  • Why real high performers wake up at 3:59 AM and treat cold showers like spiritual baptisms.
  • How to turn margin call trauma into muscle and mindset gainz.
  • Why tears are the new pre-workout (drink them, bro).
  • How to invest in NFTs before breakfast and read 12 books by dinner (or fake it with confidence).
  • Stoic philosophy, gym wisdom, and meme energy — fused into one outrageous life plan.

Packed with bro-science, brutal truths, and dangerous levels of alpha, this masterclass is half warrior’s creed, half satire — and 100% guaranteed to kick you directly into your next evolution. Whether you’re looking to level up your routine, dominate your inner beta, or just flex on mediocrity, this book will slap you in the face with motivation and call it a warm-up.
